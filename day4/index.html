WEB DEVELOPER

A web developer is a professional who specializes in creating, coding, and maintaining websites. They use various programming languages, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and others, to design, develop, and enhance websites for both personal and commercial use. Web developers can work in different roles, such as front-end developers who focus on the user interface and user experience, back-end developers who handle server-side logic and databases, or full-stack developers who work on both front-end and back-end aspects. Their main goal is to ensure websites are visually appealing, functional, and user-friendly while adhering to industry standards and best practices.
